About

azi Nazrul Islam (1899-1976) is the national poet of Bangladesh and a prominent figure in Bengali literature, known as the "rebel poet" for his writings advocating for social and political justice and opposing oppression. A poet, musician, writer, and activist, Nazrul penned works rich with themes of humanism, equality, rebellion, and spiritual devotion. His compositions, known as Nazrul Gīti, are an integral part of Bengali culture and music.
